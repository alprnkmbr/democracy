What we measure

We measure democracy not by procedures (elections, constitutions) but by whether every individual has effective power over decisions that shape their life. Three pillars: Integrated Will (are preferences genuine or manufactured?), Organic Ties (do communities protect without oppressing?), Social Capital Transformation (does wealth serve the public or extract from it?).
