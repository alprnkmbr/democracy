# Democracy Pulse

A real-time, transparent, multi-source democracy index grounded in the Grand Social Theory (GST).

## Theoretical Foundation

Democracy is measured through the lens of **Rooted Freedom** — the capacity of individuals to determine their collective and personal destiny, anchored within protective social shields that buffer them from the totalizing power of the state or capital.

### Three Pillars

1. **Integrated Will** (Psychological/Cognitive) — Intellectual autonomy from biopolitical control and cultural hegemony
2. **Organic Ties** (Sociological/Anthropological) — Health of the social fabric; distinguishing Organic Democracy from Micro-Fascism
3. **Social Capital Transformation** (Economic/Political) — Legitimacy of wealth based on its social circulation

### Cross-Cutting Rights

Integrity · Justice · Compassion · Welfare · Truth · Solidarity
