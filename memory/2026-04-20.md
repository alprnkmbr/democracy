## Government Type as Dimension (07:36)

**Decision**: Government type is a separate, visible dimension in the GST index.

- Hereditary monarchy / privileged dynasty = structural democratic deficit
- A kingdom cannot be regarded as fully democratic — constitutional order itself encodes birth-based inequality
- This is not cosmetic: sovereignty derived from the people vs. sovereignty derived from birth
- **Scoring logic**: Government type reduces score but proportionally — a well-governed constitutional monarchy scores lower than an equivalent republic, but NOT as low as authoritarian states
- **Display logic**: Shown separately and prominently — viewers must see this reality, it can't be buried
- Norway is the key test case: Freedom House scores it 100/100, but it has a king

**GST framing**: Hereditary privilege is itself a form of Forced Substitution — people "accept" the monarch because the constitutional alternative is excluded. The dynasty substitutes genuine democratic self-determination at the constitutional level.
